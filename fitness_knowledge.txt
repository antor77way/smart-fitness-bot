WORKOUT BASICS

Regular exercise improves strength, health, and mental wellness.

Basic workout rules:
Always warm up before exercise (5–10 minutes)
Start with light weight if you are beginner
Maintain correct exercise form
Do not rush exercises
Take rest between sets
Always stretch after workout

Beginner workout example:
Push-ups → Chest
Squats → Legs
Plank → Core
Walking → Cardio

Recommended workout days:
Beginner: 3 days per week
Intermediate: 4–5 days per week
Advanced: 5–6 days per week

BMI CALCULATION SYSTEM

BMI means Body Mass Index.

BMI formula:

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height² (meter)

BMI categories:

Below 18.5:
Underweight

18.5 – 24.9:
Normal weight

25 – 29.9:
Overweight

30 and above:
Obese



BMI BASED FITNESS ADVICE

If BMI below 18.5:

Focus on:
Weight gain
Strength training
High calorie diet


If BMI between 18.5 and 24.9:

Maintain routine
Balanced diet
Regular exercise


If BMI above 25:

Focus on:
Weight loss
Cardio exercise
Diet control
